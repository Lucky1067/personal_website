About me

Working at these places was fun, I learned a lot, but I cannot see myself supporting my aging parents on the wage I was making. So, I decided to go back to school. I chose CIT because I have always had an interest in computers and technology, even from a young age. I applied in the summer and thankfully got accepted into the program. In order to prepare I went through some A+ material, I also took apart my old PS3 console, applied thermal paste and then installed homebrew. I learned a bit of knowledge from this, but nothing could prepare me for the sheer volume of the information coming my way at BCIT.
